From Garden to Plate

Apotager is a French term for an ornamental vegetable or kitchen garden. Often flowers (edible an no edible) and herbs are planted with the vegetables to enchance the garden's beauty. The goal is the function of providing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for their functionality as for their color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers and herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise their function of providing for a home in a wide array forms - from the carefrre style the cottage garden to the normality of a knot garden.
